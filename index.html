Learning Pathway

What Is Learning Pathway

Learning Pathway helps to develop skills which you are interested in learning. You explore a certain pathway to improve in a area and build your confidence into the future learning vital skills for the workplace.

Choose Your Course

After you have chosen the pathway in which you would like to explore you will need to head to Future Learn to find the course that you will be studing for the next few weeks. These courses range from 2 weeks to six weeks. The longer courses will require extra effort outside of school. However the shorter courses can be completed in the lesson. These courses are simple to follow and learn from.

Research Reflections

Now that you have completed your course, it is now time to expand your knowledge on the subject as you take a look at articles and videos. These are very helpful as you are going to be presenting what you have learnt so far. This extra bit of infomation really can help your presentation excell with way more detail.
